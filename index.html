Ping - Pong

What this does:

This app either counts from one to your number, or down from your number to one. Except:

  • Numbers divisble by 3 are replaced with "ping."
  • Numbers divisible by 5 are replaced with "pong."
  • Numbers divisible by both 3 and 5 are replaced by "ping-pong"
